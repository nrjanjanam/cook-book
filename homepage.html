About Us

People generally face problems in choosing what and how they want to cook. We are also confused about where to find the perfect ingredients to make the recipe. We tend to waste our time surfing the internet or roaming around streets to various shops to get the groceries. Cookbook.com gives an all-in-one solution to these problems.

Cookbook.com is a platform where one can find various food recipes across cuisines. Here, one can read a blog/watch videos of the recipe that they are interested in. The platform also provides an added service to buy groceries required for the food they wish to prepare. If one doesn't know what they want to cook/eat, they can follow the suggestions which recommends them according to their preference of taste. It also suggests some add-ons (eg: beverages, breads, desserts, etc. ) with your recipe. The platform suggests food according to the time of the day like breakfast,lunch,snacks or dinner. Cookbook.com is a dream come true for foodies.

This application will aid people to save time and enjoy a great meal.
